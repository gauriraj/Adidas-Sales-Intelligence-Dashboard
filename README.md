# 📊 Adidas Sales Intelligence Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview
Developed a **Power BI Sales Intelligence Dashboard** for Adidas using 9,600+ retail records to analyze sales performance, profitability, and operational trends across regions, products, and sales channels.

This project focuses on converting raw business data into **actionable insights** that support strategic decision-making and performance optimization.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Retail organizations require clear visibility into sales drivers, profitability, and channel performance to remain competitive.

However, fragmented data makes it difficult to identify:
- High-performing regions and products  
- Profitability drivers  
- Underperforming sales channels  
- Opportunities for growth and optimization  

This dashboard addresses these challenges through a **centralized analytics solution**.

---

## ❓ Key Business Questions

- 📌 Which retailers and regions contribute most to total sales?
- 📊 Which product categories deliver the highest operating margins?
- 📈 How does sales performance vary across regions?
- 💰 Which regions generate maximum operating profit?
- 🛒 Which sales channels drive the highest revenue?
- 📉 Where are the performance gaps across regions and channels?

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

- 🔹 End-to-end KPI tracking (Sales, Profit, Units Sold, Margin, Pricing)
- 🔹 Retailer-level performance analysis
- 🔹 Product-wise margin and sales comparison
- 🔹 Region-wise profitability insights
- 🔹 Sales channel performance breakdown
- 🔹 State-level sales distribution (geo insights)
- 🔹 Interactive filtering for dynamic analysis

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- ✅ **West region leads in operating profit**, indicating strong regional dominance  
- 📊 **In-store channel contributes the highest share of total sales (~39%)**  
- 💰 **Top retailers such as West Gear and Foot Locker drive major revenue share**  
- 📈 **Men’s product categories contribute significantly to total unit sales**  
- ⚠️ **Midwest region underperforms compared to other regions**  
- 📉 **Online sales lag behind in-store performance, indicating optimization opportunity**
